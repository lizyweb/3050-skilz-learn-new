Understanding Basic Digital Marketing


Before diving into the specifics of a basic digital marketing course, let's first understand what digital marketing entails. All marketing initiatives that use electronic devices or the internet to connect and interact with audiences are included in the category of digital marketing. It includes various channels such as social media, email, search engines, websites, and mobile apps, each offering unique opportunities to connect with potential customers and drive business growth.
