Frequently Asked Questions

  • What courses does Skilz Learn offer?

    Skilz Learn offers a range of courses including Ui&UX Design Course, Basic Digital Marketing Course, VFX Course, game design course, and Advanced Digital Marketing Course.

  • What is the duration of the courses offered by Skilz Learn?

    The duration of each course varies. Typically, courses range from a few weeks to several months, depending on the complexity and depth of the subject matter.

  • Are the courses at Skilz Learn suitable for beginners?

    Yes, our courses are designed to cater to learners of all levels, including beginners. We offer foundational as well as advanced courses to meet the needs of students at different skill levels.

  • Do I need any prior experience to enroll in Skilz Learn courses?

    No prior experience is required for most of our courses. However, certain advanced courses may have prerequisites, which will be clearly mentioned in the course description.

  • What kind of career opportunities can I expect after completing a course at Skilz Learn?

    Our courses are designed to equip students with practical skills that are in demand in the industry. Upon completion, you can pursue careers in UI/UX design, digital marketing, VFX, game design, and related fields.

  • Are the courses at Skilz Learn accredited?

    Yes, our courses are carefully crafted by industry experts and are recognized for their quality and relevance in the industry. While we may not be formally accredited by external bodies, our focus is on providing practical, job-oriented education.

  • How are the courses at Skilz Learn delivered?

    Our courses are delivered through a combination of online lectures, hands-on projects, assignments, and interactive sessions. This flexible approach allows students to learn at their own pace from anywhere in the world.

  • Do I need to purchase any additional software or materials for the courses?

    Depending on the course, there may be recommended software or materials. However, we strive to provide as many resources as possible within the course fee. Any additional requirements will be clearly communicated beforehand.

  • Does Skilz Learn offer job placement assistance?

    Yes, we provide job placement assistance to our students upon successful completion of their courses. Our career services team works closely with industry partners to help students find relevant job opportunities.

  • Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied with the course?

    We want all our students to have a positive learning experience. If you're not satisfied with the course for any reason, please contact our support team within the specified refund period, and we'll be happy to assist you with a refund or course credit.
